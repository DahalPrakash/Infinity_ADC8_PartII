Welcome to Herald College Kathmandu (HCK)

Herald College Kathmandu (HCK)

Herald College Kathmandu provides the best UK university education at most affordable fees. It offers Bachelor Degrees in IT and Business in direct partnership with the University of Wolverhampton, UK. Programmes that Herald College Kathmandu offers are flexible and have always aimed to foster a broader based knowledge.


Herald College Kathmandu offers the BSc (Hons) International Business Management and BSc (Hons) in Computer Science qualification from the University of Wolverhampton (WLV) based on a 3+1 model, whereby Herald College Kathmandu students would first take iAcademy’s University Foundation Certification, followed by their Diploma in Business Administration or Diploma in Computing, and then their Advanced Diploma in Business Administration or Advanced Diploma in Computing. All of the iAcademy’s qualifications have been articulated with advanced standing into the University of Wolverhampton’s BSc (Hons) International Business Management degree and BSc (Hons) in Computer Science qualification. Hence, students who successfully complete iAcademy’s Advanced Diploma in Business Administration or Advanced Diploma in Computing would enter the University of Wolverhampton’s BSc (Hons) International Business Management and BSc (Hons) in Computer Science programme in the final year of that programme.


Considering effort, aptitude and knowledge, Herald College Kathmandu organizes a competition every year where students are awarded with scholarships to participate in 15-Day Summer-Camp organized at the University of Wolverhampton, UK.
